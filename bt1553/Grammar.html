NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

kind of nouns

Nonus are classified as follow:

  • Proper: It is the name of the particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.
  • Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.
  • Gollective: It is the name representing a group of things.
  • Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

pronouns

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

kind of pronoun

Prononus are classified as follow:

  • Personal: It refers to a specific noun and used as substitute of the noun.
  • Interrogative prononus: It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite prononus: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
  • Relative prononus: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same timne connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative prononus: A noun or pronoun is specified and Identified by it.
  • Reflexive prononus: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
